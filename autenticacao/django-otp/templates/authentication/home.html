Enhanced Security

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of protection beyond just passwords, making your accounts significantly more secure.

Time-Based Codes

TOTP generates unique 6-digit codes that change every 30 seconds, ensuring maximum security with time-sensitive authentication.

Mobile Compatible

Works with popular authenticator apps like Google Authenticator, Authy, and Microsoft Authenticator on any smartphone.
